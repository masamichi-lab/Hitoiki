# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

## Information We Collect

Hitoiki ("the App") does not collect any personal information from its users.

The content you record in the App (anger records, children's names, triggers, notes, feelings, actions) is stored on your device via SwiftData and synced only to your **private iCloud database** under your Apple ID. The developer cannot read or access this data.

## Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share your data with any third party.

## Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any analytics tool or tracking SDK. It does not display advertisements.

## Network Activity

The App's only network activity is sync traffic with Apple iCloud / CloudKit. The App does not communicate with any other server.

## Deleting Your Data

Removing the App from your device does not delete your iCloud copy. To delete it completely, go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud → Hitoiki → Delete.
