# Support

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, please open a GitHub issue:

- <https://github.com/masamichi-lab/Hitoiki/issues/new>

A free GitHub account is required.

## FAQ

### Where is my data stored?
Only in your private iCloud database. The developer cannot see your data.

### Can I use it on multiple devices?
Yes — sign in with the same Apple ID and your records sync automatically.

### What happens if I disable iCloud?
Your records stay on the device and are not synced anywhere else.
